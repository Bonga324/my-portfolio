5 Things I Learned from Building a Travel Wishlist App

Creating a travel wishlist app was more than just a creative outlet — it was a true learning experience in balancing design, functionality, and user expectations. Here are five key takeaways from the journey:

  • 1. Design for Emotion: I wanted the app to spark excitement and wanderlust. Using vibrant imagery and meaningful text helped me create that emotional connection.
  • 2. Smooth Budget Calculator: Travel involves planning, so I built a calculator to help users estimate their costs. Ensuring the inputs were intuitive and results clear was key.
  • 3. Responsive UI: Travelers use phones more than desktops, so I focused on mobile-first design to deliver the best experience across devices.
  • 4. Polls that Engage: I added interactive polls to learn user preferences and make the app more engaging — which also helped improve content relevance.
  • 5. Feedback = Direction: The most valuable insights came from feedback. Listening to what users actually wanted shaped the final version of the app.

Building this app pushed me to mix creativity with clarity — and always keep the user’s journey in mind.
