🌟 Featured Projects

A selection of personal and collaborative projects that showcase my passion for building practical and creative web experiences.

eConFix

eConFix is a modern digital platform that streamlines access to construction and maintenance services. It empowers users to seamlessly book skilled professionals, manage service requests, and complete secure online payments — all in one place. The platform is designed for efficiency and trust, connecting clients with qualified technicians who deliver on-site solutions with speed and quality.

Technologies: ASP.NET MVC, jQuery, HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap

Travel Wishlist Website

This vibrant travel wishlist website showcases my dream destinations: New York City, Tokyo, and Paris. It offers an interactive experience with curated travel tips, a budget calculator, and a voting poll that lets visitors choose their favorite destination. The site was built to demonstrate my passion for both travel and UI/UX design, blending functionality with beautiful aesthetics.

  • Wishlist for top destinations
  • Interactive budget calculator
  • Embedded poll for user engagement
  • Travel tips and fun facts

Technologies: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap
