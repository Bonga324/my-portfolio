Behind the Code: Building eConFix

eConFix started as an idea to connect communities with reliable construction and maintenance services — and turned into a full-featured platform with real-world impact. Here's how I brought it to life:

  • 1. Architecting the Platform: I used ASP.NET MVC to separate logic, views, and data effectively. This made it easy to maintain and expand.
  • 2. Service Coordination Logic: I built backend logic to assign the right technician based on service type and location — ensuring fast, accurate job routing.
  • 3. Online Payments: Integration of secure online payments added convenience and trust, using payment gateway APIs to handle transactions.
  • 4. Clean UI with Bootstrap: I leveraged Bootstrap for layout, combined with custom CSS to match brand colors and deliver a sleek, modern look.
  • 5. Real Feedback, Real Iteration: Feedback from users helped me refine everything — from the booking flow to technician response times.

Building eConFix wasn’t just about writing code. It was about solving problems and creating tools that make life easier — one click at a time.
